All products are delivered as downloadable PDFs. They are printable and optimized for both screen reading and physical use. The checklists include interactive checkboxes that work in most PDF readers.

Yes. All products reflect current 2026 USCIS fees, processing times, form versions, and embassy procedures. We update them whenever government requirements change and you get free updates for life.
